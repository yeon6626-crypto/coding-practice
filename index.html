Backstory

This project, BECOMING, is a study in restraint and presence—where identity meets craft. The visual system balances warmth with precision, letting typography and negative space carry the narrative.

Every decision—from grids, layout, and typography to micro-interactions—supports a single idea: elegance that feels lived-in, not staged.
